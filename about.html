About Performance Hoops

Jon Folonis founded Performance Hoops with the vision of building a skill development academy that would fill a void in the realm of individual player development. With over 5 years of training experience, Jon has introduced the Bay Area to a program that caters to individuals ages 8 years and older. We dedicate ourselves to each and every athlete who walks through ours doors and we pride ourselves on teaching the game in an enthusiastic and positive environment that demands results.

Programs

  • One-on-one training: all ages, all levels
  • Group training
  • Team training
  • Coaching clinics
  • Endurance training
  • Skills training
